Aim 2: Predicting Tau PET Positivity from Plasma Biomarkers

Overview

This analysis predicts amyloid and tau PET positivity from plasma biomarkers (ptau181, ptau217) using logistic regression. We calculate:

  • ROC curves and AUC
  • Optimal cutoffs using Youden index (sensitivity + specificity - 1)
  • Sensitivity and specificity at optimal cutoffs

Tau positivity thresholds: (PREVENT-AD cohort-specific)

  • Tau: SUVR ≥ 1.28
